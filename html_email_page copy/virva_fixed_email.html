Cleaner Environment
Electronics have toxins such as Lead, Mercury, Cadmium and Beryllium. If electronics are not disposed properly, these toxins can end up polluting our groundwater and air.
 
Throwing out electronics with the regular trash is not a responsible thing to do, and is in fact outlawed in most western countries.
